These here are old works of mine, while not front page, they were fruitful to my growth as an artist and to my being.

"Urban Crab" (2023)

A fluffy horseshoe crab imitating other animals through idling and touch. This was a collaborative experience with my mother alongside starting an small interest in fabric, but mostly about how different “materials” lead to different interactions.

"Surpasseth" (2019)

My first introduction to game development with a project in Unreal Engine 4, started my passion for game development. It is simply a third-person shooter with platforming elements and a few levels.

"Iterative P1" (2022)

Introduction to prototyping with game development, this project was one of many small prototypes. My encouragement to choose this “P1” in particular was because of a peer’s enjoyment of one segment of the game.
